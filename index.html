04 Our approach

Our core beliefs, values, and guiding principles.

I. Recovery
II. Quality
III. Education
IV. Advocacy
I · Recovery

Recovery is possible.

We believe that recovery is possible, and hope is always one step ahead. Our person-centered care planning process enables effective development of your treatment plan.

II · Quality

The highest quality of care.

We strive to provide the highest quality of care because we respect and value our individuals. Our multi-disciplinary team works together under one roof.

III · Education

To be informed is to be empowered.

Educating and informing each consumer about their mental health diagnosis and treatment options is our number one priority. We believe in spreading awareness.

IV · Advocacy

We speak for the voiceless.

We will speak for the voiceless and also empower them to use their voice for the good of mankind. Every individual participates in their treatment goals.

07 What happens next

From your first message to your first session — in one week or less.

→ No process. No waiting room of forms. Here's exactly what to expect, written by the staff who actually do it.

  1. Today

    Reach out to us

    Call (443) 438-5538 or visit us at 1 N Charles St, Baltimore. We're here Monday through Friday, 9 AM to 5 PM.

  2. 3 business days

    Meet your team

    We match you to a clinician — and a prescriber if you'd like — and you meet them before booking.

  3. Within 1 week

    First session — your way

    Baltimore office, or secure video from anywhere in Maryland. 50 minutes. You set the pace.

  4. Every 30 days

    Plan, reviewed together

    Is this working? Honest check-in, every month. We change course together when it isn't.
